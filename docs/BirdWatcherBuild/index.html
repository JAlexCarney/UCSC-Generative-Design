Bird Watcher

By J Alex Carney

.

Contact :

Joacarne@ucsc.edu

.

Bird Watcher is a walking sim type experience in which you can explore a procedurally generated world with procedurally generated wildlife. Explore using WASD to walk and the Mouse to look around. Additionally you can use E to zoom in and escape to puase input. This is a prototype and I Hope to continue working on this project, adapt it into a game about proffesional nature photography and migrate it to itch.io.
