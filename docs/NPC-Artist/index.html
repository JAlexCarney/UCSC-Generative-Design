Controls

Arrow keys to move
SpaceBar to interact
y to accept

Discription

This project uses a generative grammer to produce music. Move the pink bird over to the yellow bird and press spacebar to chat with him. He produces music using a midi-synthisizer. The grammar runs 5 generations and picks valid rules with uniform possibility.
